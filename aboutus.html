What is Vocalise?

Vocalise is a society where you can learn to sing and express yourself through music, whether you are a beginner, or a star! we would love to have you!!!! We are based in scotland however all of our events are mostly Online so anyone from anywhere can take part!. You can sign up via the contact us page.

As vocalise we like to take into acount all musical talent!! this includes instrumentalists, dj's and ofcourse, Singers!. we accept singers from all backgrounds whether its modern pop to classical, we would also love to see some of you theatre kids out there join us.
